Our Commitment

Quality in Every Component

At AURA PLAST, we challenge the conventions of tapware. Our focus is on leveraging the unique strengths of engineered polymers like PTMT to deliver products that are not just alternatives to metal, but superior in many applications.

Our taps offer exceptional resistance to corrosion and hard water scaling, ensuring longevity and consistent performance. Through rigorous R&D, we produce lightweight, robust, and cost-effective solutions without compromising on design.

How It's Made

Manufacturing Excellence

01Design & Prototyping

Our process begins in our digital lab, where we use CAD and flow simulation to design for perfect ergonomics, easy installation, and flawless water delivery.

02Polymer & Injection Molding

We use only virgin, high-grade PTMT and other polymers. Our state-of-the-art injection molding facility ensures every component is created with sub-millimeter precision.

03Quality & Global Logistics

Each tap undergoes rigorous pressure testing and a 20-point quality check. Products are then securely packaged for efficient, reliable export to our partners worldwide.
