Our Philosophy

The Art of Reduction

At AURA, we believe true luxury lies in simplicity. Our design process is one of thoughtful reduction—stripping away the superfluous to reveal the essential form. Each curve, line, and finish is deliberate, creating fixtures that are both visually silent and profoundly impactful.

We source only the finest, lead-free brass and apply robust, eco-conscious finishes to ensure every piece not only looks timeless but lasts a lifetime.

How It's Made

Our Design & Craft

01Concept & Form

Every design begins as a sketch, focused on ergonomic feel and visual harmony. We use 3D modeling to refine every millimeter before a single piece is forged.

02Material & Craft

We cast our fixtures from solid, lead-free brass for its heft and durability. Our artisans then meticulously machine and hand-polish each component to perfection.

03Finish & Assembly

A multi-layer PVD finishing process ensures a resilient, corrosion-proof surface. Finally, each piece is hand-assembled and pressure-tested to guarantee flawless performance.
