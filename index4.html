What is PTMT?

Polytetra Methylene Terephthalate (PTMT) is an engineered plastic that offers the best of both worlds. Launched in India within the last decade, it has quickly become the preferred choice for premium plastic taps and sanitary materials.

Its unique properties provide exceptional durability against environmental stress, preventing cracks and scratches, while maintaining a lightweight and easy-to-clean profile.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between PVC and PTMT?

While both are polymers, PTMT is considered an "engineered plastic" produced via condensation polymerization. This gives it superior hardness, a glossy finish, and higher resistance to environmental stress and scratches compared to standard PVC.

What are the main advantages of plastic taps?

Vectus PTMT taps are resistant to corrosion, require less energy to produce and transport, and are incredibly durable. Their robust nature, combined with tensile strength and abrasion resistance, makes them a perfect, long-lasting fit for any plumbing system.

How do I clean my Vectus plastic tap?

Cleaning is simple. Use a mild soap solution or a mix of vinegar and water. Gently scrub with a soft cloth to remove any dirt. Avoid using harsh chemical cleaners or abrasive pads to maintain the tap's brilliant shine and longevity.
